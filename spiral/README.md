# Spiral Clock

Time does not have 12 hours nor is it a flat circle. As each hour passes, it brings with it the next, just like how each day follows the previous day.

The Spiral Clock visualizes time as an infinite inward spiral that continuously zooms in as the seconds tick by. The spiral employs a relativistic color shift to help you perceive the passage of time. The present moment glows white. The past stretches outward in red, receding like distant galaxies. The future slowly approaches in blue. A meditation on causality.
